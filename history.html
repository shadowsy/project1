Dec 2005

Jointly Establish a Confucius Institute

To participate in the "1 + 2 + 1 the talent cultivation plan of began to work together to set up Confucius institute, China university of hebei normal university of science and technology is to participate in the project of university of China and the United States and the United States Troy university established the first" Confucius institute ". On October 11, 2007 in hebei province vice governor Mr Long Zhuangwei and the United States Alabama governor Bob riley Mr Attend at the two schools of China and the United States in Beijing set up "signing ceremony of Confucius institute".

Participating in the 1 + 2 + 1 talent training program, Chinese and American universities are actively contacting and preparing for the establishment of more Confucius institutes in the United States.

Oct 11, 2007

Communication Stage

From 2004 to 2008, Chinese universities sent several backbone teachers to study in American universities every year, and some of these backbone teachers have been promoted into leading positions in Chinese universities, and some have started to take up bilingual teaching tasks in Chinese universities. Meanwhile, since 2005, many American universities have sent teachers to Chinese universities for exchange and study.

2008

Cooperation Stage

From 2007 to 2012, we held the "seminar on cooperation in the construction of tourism disciplines of Chinese and American universities", "seminar on cooperation in the construction of economics disciplines of Chinese and American universities" and "seminar on cooperation in the construction of engineering disciplines of Chinese and American universities". Experts and professors from dozens of Chinese and American universities attended the meeting, held serious discussions on tourism, economics and engineering, and reached an intention on cooperation between Chinese and American universities in tourism, economics and engineering.

2012
