Hostfriend Program

    American universities have a large community of international students. Allowing international students homestay with participating community members gives students the opportunity to acclimate to daily American life and discover new customs in a safe environment outside of the University community. Additionally, hosts keep in touch with their student throughout their first semester.

Academic Life

    121 project is a good platform for learning and communication. Students can study abroad through the program. The 121 program of changshu institute of technology is the American university of uwec. The good learning environment and atmosphere there allow students to broaden their horizons. Students of project 121 will study in the United States for two years, meet the degree requirements and graduation requirements of the two universities, and can obtain the bachelor's degree and Chinese graduation certificate from the two universities. Students of the program have a diploma and degree from Chinese and American universities and a good record of entering and leaving the United States. More than 70% of our graduates will study in the top 50 universities in the United States.
