About 121 Program

    121 program is in the China association of education international exchange (CEAIE), international education exchange center (CCIEE) and the American association of state university and college work (AASCU) and management of the new type of higher education of China and the United States two-way exchanges and cooperation projects, choose by CCIEE and AASCU conditions of China and the United States public colleges and universities as a member of the program, specific teaching task.

How does it work?

    The cooperation of CIT with university of Wisconsin at Eau Claire, university of Minnesota Duluth,Southern Illinois university edwardsville, Troy university hold college credits mutual recognition of joint training program (double degree), using 2 + 1.5 + 0.5 training mode, program according to the university students to develop joint training plan should be to study for two years, the project students in the first three semesters at changshu institute of technology, the first four to the seventh semester must work in the United States university two years, return the eighth term changshu institute of technology, accord with requirement of a bachelor's degree in American university courses completed, bachelor's degree from the university, the courses can meet the demands of changshu institute of technology and a bachelor's degree graduation requirements, May obtain changshu institute of technology bachelor's degree certificate, bachelor's degree certificate. If the student cannot go to study in the United States, he/she shall be regarded as abandoning the joint training program of mutual recognition of credits between China and foreign countries (double degree), and the student shall drop out.
